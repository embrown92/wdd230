LOVING LOCAL IS MORE IMPORTANT THAN EVER

This holiday season looks a little different, but we can still share joy. Shop small and support your favorite small businesses. Loving local bolsters the local economy since for every $100 spent at locally owned businesses, $68 will stay in the community, whereas, only $43 remains in the community when shopping at a national chain, according to the Independent We Stand organization.

MEMBER SPOTLIGHT

Kelly’s Homemade Ice Cream

Kelly’s Homemade Ice Cream is a local, handcrafted ice cream shop located inside Foxtail Coffee Co. Its counter service offers a wide selection of creamy favorites from the classics to adventurous flavors such as Ore-Dough and Banana Pudding. All of Kelly’s homemade ice creams and sorbets are uniquely created flavors, made locally using high quality ingredients. Non-dairy options are also available.
